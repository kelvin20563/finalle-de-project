Our Coffee Journey

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small passion project between friends who shared a love for exceptional coffee. Today, we've grown into a community hub where coffee enthusiasts gather to savor carefully crafted beverages.

We source our beans directly from ethical farms across South America and Africa, roasting them in-house to bring out their unique characteristics. Every cup is brewed with precision and care by our skilled baristas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cafe, coffee, and services.

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website, by calling us directly at (555) 123-4567, or by visiting our cafe in person. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for weekend brunches and special events.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a variety of gluten-free pastries and snacks available daily. Our menu clearly marks gluten-free items, and our staff is trained to answer any dietary questions you may have. Please note that while we take precautions, our kitchen does handle gluten-containing ingredients.

Where do you source your coffee beans?

We partner directly with small scale farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Brazil who practice sustainable farming. Our beans are ethically sourced through Fair Trade agreements, and we roast them in-house weekly to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.
